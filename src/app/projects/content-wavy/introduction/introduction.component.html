Title: Wavy, Should I Invest in? Role: User Experience Researcher and User Timeline to Develop MVP: 29 October 2021 - 05 December 2021. Wavy is a web application that allows users to build a profitable investment portfolio. How? Wavy allows users to identify which listed investment products best suits their financial objectives. All the user has to do is search the investment product that they wish to learn more about. With A simple Wavy search users will be provided with, the average annual return, the risk profile, potential dividend earnings, the potential tax implications and the expected performance of the searched financial product. Furthermore, they will also be provided with the potential returns they can expect from the product. All this information gives the user access to fundamental information about listed financial products, that will empower them in making informed investment decisions. Wavy is designed to function as the leading platform that aids in transforming everyday consumers into knowledgeable capital market investors.
